About Us

NuviTek is a results driven and customer focused solutions provider that provides services in Cloud Computing, IT Infrastructure, Datacenter Consolidation, Virtualization and Big Data Infrastructure.

Our Work

Our clients love what we do and so will you.

Case Study: Datacenter Transformation

One of our Federal Customers datacenter was experiencing physical server sprawl with growing demand for additional server capacity to develop, test and deploy new applications. Adding new servers to meet this demand meant adding additional real estate, power and cooling to the datacenter. The time required to provision and deliver servers to the application team was significantly high which caused delays in several projects implementations.

Learn more

Case Study: Datacenter Migration

One of our commercial clients acquired another company and in the process acquired additional data center resources. Consolidation of resources within these data centers was a requirement and moving virtual machines and applications from the new data center to the existing primary corporate data center was necessary.

Learn more

Case Study: Desktop Virtualization

Nuvitek was awarded a contract by a Federal customer to train users in multiple locations. In a traditional classroom training setup, desktop PCs are made available to students in dedicated classrooms, which takes valuable space as well as maintaining PCs with different specifications. This is both time consuming and costly. In addition, trainers have to travel to each location and setup the classroom environment which is a significant logistical challenge and expense.

Learn more

Case Study: Big Data Infrastructure

One of our Federal customers required a search solution that would crawl and index gigabytes of data in a short time frame. The existing search solution was taking weeks to crawl and index. We built a cloud based Big Data Infrastructure with Hadoop, Apache Nutch and Apache Solr. By implementing our Solution, customer was able to crawl and index the data in less than 8 hours compared to weeks.
